About the Studio
​

Our studio is located in the former Videolab of Philips in Eindhoven (The Netherlands), center of the so called ‘Brainport‘. Over the last years, a team of 15 developers, designers and artist has formed to work on the Pillow’s Willow universe, also known as the virtual dreamscape.

Our aim is to offer people VR (and in a later stage also AR) dreamscapes, where they can take a break from the real world and be astonished by wonderful virtual environments where they can look around, play, have fun and feel good. Let people play & enjoy wonderful, constructive and positive VR experiences!
